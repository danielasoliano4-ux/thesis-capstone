Emergency First Aid for Animal Bites
1

Wash the wound immediately

Use soap and running water for at least 15 minutes

2

Apply antiseptic solution

Use povidone-iodine, alcohol, or hydrogen peroxide

3

Do NOT cover the wound

Leave it open to prevent infection

4

Seek medical attention immediately

Go to the nearest clinic within 24 hours

CRITICAL WARNING

Rabies is 100% fatal once symptoms appear. Early treatment is essential for survival.
